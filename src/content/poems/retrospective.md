Only through the   
Eventually vicarious  
Mediation of a partially solidified network of intertwined,  
Ever circulating references, and images,  
And ultimately signs,  
Can two beings every connect.

Folding back on one another.  
In the redundant interplay of the same sign,  
Of the same obsession for the same sign,

Of itself.

Leaves the possibility for a connection.
